Geneo & OxyPods

Every Geneo facial begins with a unique, patented reaction between our Geneo OxyPods and Geneo Primer Gel, which simultaneously exfoliates and oxygenates the skin. Geneo ultrasound increases absorption of highly effective ingredients from Geneo Serums, encouraging cellular renewal. Lastly, a relaxing massage seals in the treatment for maximum absorption and results.

OxyGeneo also features a gentle, yet thorough simultaneous exfoliation. The vibrating applicator can be adjusted for intensity of exfoliation, and the OxyPod itself will begin to break down into tiny granules, further enhancing the mechanical removal of dead skin cells. This combination of oxygen and exfoliation creates optimal conditions for the infusion of nutrients into the skin, which we achieve in the following steps 2 and 3 via Ultrasound and Neo-Massage. The increased skin permeability following step 1 makes homogenous absorption of active ingredients exponentially higher, and therefore more effective.

Step one

OxyGeneo

Geneo's patented oxygenation technology triggers the body's natural, physiological process that increases oxygen levels internally. This triggers the body's natural, physiological process of increased oxygen levels internally

Step two

Ultrasound

In the next step of the facial, waves stimulate microvibrations in the skin, delivering instant smoothing results on the surface and increasing the permeability of the skin`s protective layer, resulting in enhanced absorption of active ingredients.

Step three

Neo-massage

The facial finishes with the Neo-Massage step, locking in the power of Geneo. The neo-massage step delivers instant reduction in redness and puffiness while continuing to deeply infuse Geneo serum's active ingredients.
